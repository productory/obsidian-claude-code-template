# 20-work

Work workspace for OKR tracking, weekly planning, meetings, and project management.

## Contents

- **20.01-okr/** — Yearly OKR file with quarterly and monthly breakdown
- **20.02-weekly/** — Weekly 1Thing plans, priorities, and reviews
- **20.03-meetings/** — Meeting notes (dynamic, tagged, linked to projects/weeklies)
- **20.04-projects/** — Lightweight PM with PIC (Person In Charge)
- **20.05-archive/** — Completed or inactive work items
